# Application of Programming in Various Industries

## 1. Healthcare
- **Epic Systems** — electronic health record management system.
- **Teladoc** — telemedicine platform for online doctor consultations.

## 2. Finance
- **PayPal** — online payment system for transfers and payments.
- **Robinhood** — app for investing and trading stocks.

## 3. Education
- **Coursera** — online platform with courses from universities and companies.
- **Duolingo** — app for learning foreign languages.

## 4. Entertainment
- **Netflix** — streaming service for watching movies and TV shows.
- **Spotify** — music service for online listening.

## 5. Transportation
- **Uber** — app for calling taxis and ride-sharing.
- **Bolt** — taxi and e-scooter service.
